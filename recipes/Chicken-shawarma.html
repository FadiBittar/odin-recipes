Shawarma

Descreption

chicken pieces in a sandwich

Ingredients

  • large garlic clove
  • seven spices
  • chicken
  • olive oil
  • lemon

Steps to prepare

  1. Put the chicken with the spices in a bowl
  2. after 15 minutes add the lemon and a spoon of olive oil
  3. Grill the chicken for 18 minutes
  4. Put the mayonnaise and the chicken in a bread and make a sandwich
