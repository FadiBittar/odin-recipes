Four Seasons Pizza

Descreption

A circle of deadly taste pizza with anything a human being could ask for in a pizza

Ingredients

  • pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mushrooms
  • Olives
  • pepper slices
  • Meat
  • Cheese

Steps to prepare

  1. Put the tomato sauce on the dough
  2. Spread the olive , pepper ,cheese and meat each in a quarter
  3. Put the pizza in the oven for 20 minutes
  4. Put it aside til it is cold enought to EAT!!
